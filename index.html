How It Works

Three steps to match intelligence.

01

Upload your MP4

Drop your match recording. Fixed camera behind the back wall. 360p–1080p supported.

02

AI analyses the match

YOLOv8 tracks both players and the ball. Kalman smoothing. Rallies segmented automatically.

03

Download your report

Heatmaps, zone breakdowns, speed charts, rally timelines, and a PDF — ready in minutes.

Features

Everything you need to improve.

Player Heatmaps

Gaussian heatmaps showing where each player spent time. T-zone dominance and court coverage.

Ball Tracking

YOLOv8 + MOG2 motion detection + Kalman smoothing for robust ball trajectories across every rally.

Rally Segmentation

Automatic rally detection from ball-lost gaps. Per-rally stats: duration, shots, distance covered.

Zone Breakdown

9-zone court grid analysis. See which zones you dominate and which you neglect.

Speed & Distance

Per-player distance, average speed, peak speed, and time-series speed charts.

PDF Report

All charts compiled into a shareable, printable PDF. Show your coach or track progress.
